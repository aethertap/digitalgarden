## Worked examples
$$\begin{align}

\sum_n in_{n} &= 180(n-2) & \text{by dividing into triangles}\\
\sum_n ext_{n} &= \frac{360n - 2\sum_{n}in_{n}}{2} & \text{vertical angle pairs, supplementary angles}\\
\sum_n ext_{n} &= 180n - \sum_{n}in_{n}\\
&= 180n-180(n-2) & \text{Transitive PoE}\\
&= 180n - 180n + 360\\
&= 360 \degree &\text{QED}\\

\end{align}
$$
