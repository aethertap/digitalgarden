>> [!info]+ Lesson Content
>> 
>> - The distributive property 
>> -  factoring expressions 
>> Factoring is just distributive process being run backwards. If you have $c = (a + b) * d$, then factoring would be how you'd recover the values of $(a+b)$ and $d$.
